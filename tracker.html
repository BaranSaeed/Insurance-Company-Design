TECHNOLOGY

Vehicle Tracking, fleet management and theft recovery services for vehicles have received wide acceptance in Pakistan. These services are being used nationwide in time critical environments to reduce risks, add revenue assurance and stop revenue leakages owing to vehicle theft, misuse and abuse. These services enable for Insurance Companies a heightened sense of reduced risks simultaneously enabling new revenue streams and a wider product portfolio. The methods employed internationally for GPS based vehicle tracking and fleet management services involve Vehicle Tracking Device and Network Monitoring System.


1. Vehicle Tracking Device

Vehicle Tracking device receive location information (latitude, longitude) from GPS satellites. All this information (Location + Other Sensors) is then communicated via GSM networks to the Network Monitoring Systems for Information processing and reporting. Being a vital part of the service infrastructure, AskTrack performed much research and trials, best product was chosen for the Pakistani market. This product has been tailored to suit the Pakistani market. Pakistani market differs largely from the general use case for vehicle tracking in European nations. AGICO tracking device is the most efficient and durable product suiting Pakistani Mobile Networks brilliantly. Our offering is ISO Certified and has passed all necessary standardization procedures and tests as Vibration Test, Humidity Test, Shock Test and Temperature Test.


2. Vehicle Tracking Network Monitoring Sub-system (VT-NMS)

The Network Monitoring System is the back-bone of a Vehicle Tracking Network, AskTrack has employed only the best the industry had to offer to run its vehicle tracking operations center. AskTrack network monitoring system comprises of three main components; the Vehicle Tracking Communication Server and Active Tracking Module.


VEHICLE TRACKING COMMUNICATION SERVER

The communication server is a coordinator for the Vehicle Tracking Network. This server is responsible for carrying out all communications between the vehicle tracking network and the mobile vehicle tracking devices.

3. Features

An advanced vehicle security system, which reports directly with alarm details and GPS location through SMS text messaging and lets live-track the car over online map or PC tracking program. If vehicle has been moved or the battery has been cut off, control room will receive instant notification with street name and other location details. Besides, the location of car can be interrogated through SMS or view it on a map from Internet Website or GPRS phone at any time. The Tracker Device comes with an SOS Emergency button, which can be pressed in case of emergencies, accidents or hijacking. Geo Fence (Area restriction) can be set on each unit. In case a vehicle drives out of the marked area immediately an alert is generated in control room.
